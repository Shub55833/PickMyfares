ABOUT PICKMYFARES LTD

PickMyFares LTD is an online flight booking platform dedicated to helping travelers find affordable domestic and international airline tickets.

Our mission is to provide a secure, transparent, and customer-friendly booking experience while offering competitive airfare deals from multiple airlines.

We act as a travel booking intermediary between passengers and airlines and assist customers with flight reservations and travel-related support.

BOOKING POLICY

All flight bookings are subject to airline availability, fare rules, and ticket confirmation.

Airfares displayed on the website are subject to change until the ticket has been issued successfully.

Customers are responsible for reviewing all booking details carefully before completing payment.

PASSENGER RESPONSIBILITY

Passengers are solely responsible for providing correct and accurate information during booking including:

  • Full name as per passport or government-issued ID
  • Date of birth
  • Passport and visa information
  • Email address and contact details
  • Travel dates and destination information

PickMyFares LTD shall not be held responsible for any losses, denied boarding, missed flights, visa rejection, cancellation charges, or additional expenses caused due to incorrect information provided by the passenger.

It is the passenger’s responsibility to verify all travel details before confirming the reservation.

AIRLINE RESPONSIBILITY

Flight schedules, delays, cancellations, baggage policies, seat availability, and operational decisions are managed solely by the respective airline.

PickMyFares LTD acts only as a booking intermediary and shall not be liable for airline operational issues beyond our control.

PRICE POLICY

All prices displayed on our website are subject to change at any time without prior notice until ticket issuance is completed.

In rare cases of technical errors, pricing mistakes, or airline fare updates, PickMyFares LTD reserves the right to cancel the booking and provide a full refund of the amount paid.

CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

Refund eligibility depends entirely on the fare rules and policies of the respective airline.

Some tickets may be fully non-refundable or partially refundable. Service fees charged by PickMyFares LTD may remain non-refundable.

Cancellation requests must be submitted before the scheduled departure time. No-show passengers may not qualify for refunds.

Approved refunds may take between 7–21 business days depending on the airline and payment provider.

CHANGES & MODIFICATIONS

Flight date changes, itinerary modifications, and name corrections are subject to airline policies and additional charges.

Certain airline tickets may not allow changes after booking confirmation.

PAYMENT POLICY

  • We accept major credit cards and debit cards.
  • All payments are processed securely through authorized gateways.
  • Bookings are confirmed only after successful payment verification.
  • In case of payment failure or verification issues, bookings may be cancelled automatically.

TRAVEL DOCUMENTS

Passengers are responsible for carrying valid passports, visas, identification documents, and complying with destination country travel requirements.

PickMyFares LTD shall not be responsible if a passenger is denied boarding or denied entry due to missing or incorrect travel documents.

LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

PickMyFares LTD shall not be liable for indirect losses, missed connections, weather disruptions, airline bankruptcy, government restrictions, strikes, pandemics, or events beyond our control.

Our maximum liability shall not exceed the amount paid by the customer for the booking made through our platform.

PRIVACY POLICY

We may collect customer information including name, email address, phone number, payment details, and booking information for reservation processing and customer support purposes.

Customer information is protected using secure systems and is shared only with airlines, payment processors, and authorized travel suppliers when necessary.

We do not sell or misuse customer personal information.

COOKIES

Our website may use cookies and tracking technologies to improve website performance and user experience.

GOVERNING LAW

CONTACT INFORMATION
